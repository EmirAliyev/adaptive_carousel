2024 Speaker Announcement

Jim Wahlberg will join CATS 2024

—
Jim is the fifth oldest Wahlberg. Like his brothers Donnie and Mark, Jim recovered from his tough upbringing in the streets of Dorchester to become a producer, writer, and director of films. Jim is the executive director of the Mark Wahlberg Youth Foundation, created to improve the quality of life for inner city youth through a working partnership with other youth organizations.
—
His recently published autobiography, The Big Hustle, tells how in prison he was befriended by a priest-chaplain, Fr. Jim Fratus, who had a major impact on his life. It was also while he was in prison that Mother Teresa visited and he heard her offer some “life-changing” words. He received the Sacrament of Confirmation while still an inmate, and began to learn and embrace the Catholic faith. Jim and his wife live in South Florida and have three children.
